Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a bug or issue?
Email us at info@hatchgrid.in with a description of the issue, the app you're using, your device model, and OS version. Screenshots are helpful.

Send an email to info@hatchgrid.in from your registered email address with the subject "Account Deletion Request." We will process your request within 7 business days.
